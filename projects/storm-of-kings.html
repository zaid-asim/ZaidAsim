Grand Strategy & Dynastic Politics

Storm of Kings elevates standard 2D RPG dynamics by weaving a deep, systemic layer of grand strategy and historical intrigue inspired by medieval Indian history. Players do not just command heroes; they build empires, secure trade routes, and navigate the treacherous politics of rival dynasties.

🏰 Medieval Indian Grand Strategy

Step into a simulated medieval subcontinent where raw steel meets refined diplomacy. Players manage provinces, construct fortifications, control supply lines, and raise massive armies consisting of royal infantry, war elephants, and swift cavalry.

Terrain matters. Launching attacks across dry riverbeds, monsoon-swollen marshes, or dense deciduous forests dynamically alters movement speed, tactical flanking, and unit combat effectiveness.

Strategic Resource Allocation

  • 🌾 Granaries & Rations Supplies armies during siege campaigns
  • ⛓️ Iron & Armories Upgrades military tier and structural defense
  • 🪙 Silk Road Trade Passive gold tax via dynamic market routes

Dynastic Betrayal Logic

"Befriend a king today, but prepare for his arrows tomorrow. AI leaders remember every broken promise, empty cup, and crossed blade."
When you execute a captured rival general instead of ransoming them, their family lineage carries a multi-generational grudge, manifesting as trade embargoes or sudden backstabs.

🤝 The Betrayal Matrix & AI Grudges

Empires are won by treaties as much as swords. Our custom Betrayal Matrix simulates court politics and dynamic alliances. Regional AI warlords assess your kingdom's military power, historical reliability, and geographic borders.

Breaking a non-aggression pact decreases your empire's global honor metric, making future deals far costlier. Warlords react organically to your choices, forming temporary coalitions to suppress your expansion or proposing tactical marriage alliances to integrate territory peacefully.
